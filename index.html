OUR ACTIVITIES

We are a lucrative organization with profits capphed at 50%. The surplus is given to our humanitarian branch.
Our activities are divided in three main branches.

Finance

Our finance activities consists to invest the capital of our funds in different sectors. We aim to have positions in real estate, technology, Industry Water and Energy, country bonds, commodities, insurance, banking and many more possible. Our main objective is at term, to build a intern commonwealth for the companies which are part of our portfolio.

Multimedia

Our multimedia activities is composed of our productions activities, which consists to produce movies, talk show, web series and many more. It is also composed of a 24h/7 broadcast media based on a large network of journalist which have for mission to deliver accurate informations about important topic. We also have a consulting image firm which manage the image of well-knowned person in business, acting, sports, and politics.

Social

We are commited to help reduce inequalities between countries. This is why our members give ten percent of their annual revenue which is used to build infrastructure in developing countries. We are building hospitals, energy infrastructures, hydraulic infrastructures, orphanages and schools to promote equality for every lives. With these actions, we impact lives and help to promote a new image for multinational organizations.
